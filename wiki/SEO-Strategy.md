# 🔍 SEO Strategy

This document outlines the Search Engine Optimization efforts and structured data implemented to rank Master Calisthenics India locally.

## 🎯 Current Keyword Targets
Our primary focus is dominating local search in Mira Road and broader Mumbai:
- `Calisthenics Gym in Mira Road`
- `Master Calisthenics India`
- `Bodyweight training gym Mira Road`
- `Gymnastics near me`
- `Functional fitness Mumbai`
- `Best strength conditioning gym Mira Road`

## 📊 JSON-LD Schema Structures
We use embedded `application/ld+json` on our homepage to explicitly communicate to Google what our business is.

### 1. GymOrFitnessCenter Schema (Local Business)
Provides Google with our rich local data:
- **Business Details**: Name ("Master Calisthenics India"), Primary image, Price range.
- **Contact & Location**: Postal Address linked to `Mira Road East, Mumbai`, complete with precise Latitude (`19.2812`) and Longitude (`72.8561`).
- **Opening Hours**: Monday to Saturday, 06:00 to 22:00.
- **Offers Integration**: Signals our pricing structure directly to search engines (Group Batch at ₹2500, PT at ₹16000, Kids Fitness at ₹2000).

### 2. FAQPage Schema
Captures "People Also Ask" queries on Google:
- *"What is Calisthenics?"*
- *"Do I need experience to join?"*
- *"Is there a trial class?"*
By mapping our QA structure to `FAQPage` schema, we increase our chances of generating rich snippets directly on the SERP (Search Engine Results Page).

## 🗃️ Additional Technical SEO
- Canonical URLs set across all pages to prevent duplicate content flags.
- Comprehensive Open Graph (Facebook/Instagram) and Twitter Card tags.
- Secure CSP headers and mobile-first responsive design to improve Google Core Web Vitals.
