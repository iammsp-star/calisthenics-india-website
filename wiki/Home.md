# 🏛️ Master Calisthenics Elite - Wiki

Welcome to the official Wiki for the **Master Calisthenics Elite** repository. This documentation serves as a central hub for future developers, gym managers, and coaches to understand our systems, business logic, and tech infrastructure.

## 🎯 Our Mission
**Train Smart • Move Better • Live Strong**

Master Calisthenics India is the #1 calisthenics & bodyweight training gym in Mira Road, Mumbai. Our goal is to help individuals build real strength, mobility, and endurance through professional calisthenics & functional fitness training.

## 🦅 The "Elite" Training Philosophy
Our core training philosophy revolves around these four pillars:
- **Skill-Based Progression**: We focus on learning skills (Handstands, Muscle-Ups), not just burning calories.
- **Mobility First**: Joint health is our priority. We train to move freely without pain.
- **Bodyweight Mastery**: Learning to control your body to control your environment.
- **Long-Term Fitness**: Building sustainable habits over quick fixes.

## 💻 Tech Stack
The project is built on a modern, fast, and scalable stack:
- **Vercel**: Handles our hosting and edge deployments, providing fast global content delivery for our frontend.
- **Supabase**: Our open-source PostgreSQL database backend, handling `trial_bookings`, member directory syncs, and real-time data needs.
- **Razorpay**: Handles our secure checkout integration and payment gateways for memberships and one-time offers.
- **Google Apps Script**: Connects our Google Sheets CRM directly to the live website (e.g., syncing active members to Supabase).
